About Kharkiv National Automobile and Highway University (KhNAHU)

Established in 1930, Kharkiv National Automobile and Highway University (KhNAHU) is a prominent public institution in Kharkiv, Ukraine, specializing in automotive and road construction engineering. Originally founded as the Kharkiv Automobile and Highway Institute (KhAHI), it has evolved into a leading center for technical education and research.

KhNAHU offers a diverse range of programs across six faculties, including Automobile Transport, Mechanical Engineering, Road Construction, Transport Systems, Business and Management, and Mechatronics. The university is renowned for its practical training facilities, such as a unique testing field for road-construction machinery and a Laboratory of Racing Cars.

With a commitment to international collaboration, KhNAHU participates in programs like Erasmus+ and the European Tempus-EANET project, partnering with universities across Europe. The institution's emphasis on innovation and quality education has earned it the highest level of accreditation in Ukraine.
